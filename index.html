Why Vishwakarma University

  • Preparing students for various career avenues in the Fashion Industry. This includes Designers, Fashion Merchandisers, and Managers in Fashion Production Units, Fashion Stylist, Fashion Retail Buyers and Visual merchandisers
  • Familiarizing students with the present day developments in terms of technology and systems approach in the Fashion Industry
  • Nurturing logical thinking and problem solving approach in the students
  • Enabling entrepreneurial ventures by equipping them with the necessary entrepreneurial skills
  • Developing mindset to value applied research in the domain areas and to encourage graduating students to opt for further education and research
